Banana Bread Mug

Kidney beans are the star of the show in this creamy curry recipe! They're high in protein, folate, iron and phosphorous, and overall this curry offers a considerable amount of iron per serving.

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups (300 g) uncooked Japanese short-grain rice
  • 2 cups (370 g) cooked chickpeas
  • ¼ cup (36 g) chopped pickles
  • ¼ cup (60 mL) vegan mayonnaise
  • 3 Tbsp (30 g) minced onion
  • 2 Tbsp (30 mL) ketchup
  • ½ cucumber, thinly sliced using a potato peeler
  • ½ cup (35 g) shredded red cabbage
  • avocado
  • 6 nori seaweed sheets
  • 6 butter lettuce leaves

Directions

  1. To a microwave-safe mug, mash the banana with a fork until smooth.
  2. Add the milk, oil, vanilla, and salt and mix.
  3. Add the flour, flaxseed, sugar, chocolate, and baking powder and mix until just incorporated. Be careful not to over-mix.
  4. Microwave at high power (800 - 900 W) for 2 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the centre comes out clean. The cooking time may vary depending on your microwave and settings. If the cake isn't cooked through yet, simply place it back in the microwave at intervals of 15 seconds until done.
  5. Top with sliced banana, chocolate shavings, and toasted chopped pecans if desired. Enjoy!
